# HR-Data-Analysis-Attrition-Insights
This project presents a detailed HR analytics dashboard designed to help organizations understand employee demographics, attrition trends, and department-level insights.

## Project Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to analyze HR datasets and uncover insights related to:

* Employee demographics
* Attrition trends
* Salary patterns
* Department-level performance
* Job roles and monthly income
* Average salary hikes
* Age group distribution

This enables HR teams to take data-driven decisions for retention, salary planning, and workforce management.

## Key Insights From the Dashboard

### 1. Employee Demographics

* Average Age : 37 years
* Total Employees : 1470
* Average Salary : 6.5K
* Average Rating : 3.15

### 2. Attrition Overview

* Total Attrition : 237 employees
* Gender Split:
  * Male: **60%**
  * Female: **40%**

### 3. Monthly Income by Job Role
* Highest earning roles:
  * Manager : 17K
  * Research Director : 16K
* Lowest earning roles:
  * Sales Representative / Laboratory Technician : 3K

### 4. Education Field vs Attrition
* Life Sciences has the highest attrition (89 employees)
* Lowest attrition is from Human Resources (7 employees)

### 5. Salary Hike by Department
* Research & Development : 15.29%
* Sales : 15.10%
* Human Resources : 14.76%

### 6. Age Group Distribution
* Highest employee count from 20–30 age group
